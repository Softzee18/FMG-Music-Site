PARAMANCHI

Paramanchi is dedicated to sharing unique sounds and creative energy with the world. His musical journey began as a teenager, experimenting with beats and melodies on his laptop. Quickly becoming a fan favorite, Paramanchi’s music gained recognition on local radio stations and social media, leading to several acclaimed albums and singles featured in TV shows and movies.

His artistry is defined by a passion for self-expression and personal growth, constantly pushing creative boundaries with every new project. The debut single, ‘Ayo’, followed by ‘Fine Wine’ and the ‘Gentleman’ EP, marked the start of a remarkable career.

According to his manager, Olamilekan Abodunrin, the ‘Gratitude’ EP is the culmination of Paramanchi’s tireless efforts to create a sound that honors his heritage while appealing to a global audience. The project explores themes of love, self-empowerment, and African pride, with lyrics that are both deeply personal and universally relatable.

Tracks like ‘Drip On Drip’ and the lead single ‘Addicted’ showcase Paramanchi’s mission to move listeners, blending infectious rhythms with heartfelt messages of appreciation and self-love. His music celebrates the simple joys of life, the beauty of being human, and the power of gratitude—making Paramanchi an artist to watch in the Afrobeats scene.
